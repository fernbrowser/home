Setup

Connect your AI key in 10 seconds.

Paste your OpenRouter key below on iPhone or Mac Safari. iOS will offer to save it to your Passwords app, tagged to this site — and once it's there, Fern Browser on your watch can AutoFill it without you ever typing again.

This form runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded — the key you paste only triggers iOS Safari's "Save Password" prompt for this domain. We never see it.

Almost there.

Look for the "Save Password?" prompt at the top of Safari, then tap Save Password. After that, open Fern Browser on your watch, tap the API Key field, and choose this entry from the Passwords sheet.

Privacy

Your data doesn't go anywhere it shouldn't.

Built so that you can use AI on your wrist without trading away your information. Nothing on a server we don't control — because there is no server we control.

  • Your keys never leave the watch. Stored in Apple Keychain, locked to your device.
  • No analytics. No tracking. No advertising SDKs. None.
  • Chats go straight to OpenRouter. Fern has no server in the middle.
  • History stays local. Bookmarks and search history are saved only on your wrist.
